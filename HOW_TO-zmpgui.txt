IN TERMINAL #1:

    Open the ach channel
        ../run.sh achrestart

    Start the gui
        ./zmpgui

IN TERMINAL #2

    Start the zmp-daemon
        ./zmp-daemon ../myhubo.kinbody.xml

    In the zmp-daemon program you can using the following walk commands
        i: walk forward in a straight line
        k: stop
        m: walk backwards in a straight line
        j: sidestep to the left
        l: sidestep to the right
        u: turn more to the left while walking forward or backwards
        o: turn more to the right while walking forward or backwards

    NOTE:
        Before switching walk type, stop ('k') and let Hubo walk
        to a stop before pressing the next command.
